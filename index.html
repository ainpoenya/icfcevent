Dear Colleagues,

The majority of the energy depends on the fossil fuel and it used naturally as electicity power and heat in daily life. Many countries and companies have invested heavily on extensive research and development programs in recent years devoted to developing new alternative and renewable energy sources. Hydrogen energy and fuel cells are the most promising green and clean alternative energy sources and energy conversion device respectively for the future because they have low or zero emission and environmental friendly. Therefore, demand for hydrogen and fuel cell systems has increased as the cost of the technologies has come down and awareness of their economic and environmental benefits has increased. Realizing the fast development of polymer materials as well as fuel cell and hydrogen technology, in order to get idea about the current status of polymer material, fuel cell and hydrogen technology development, Indonesian Polymer Association (HPI) and Indonesian Association for Fuel Cell and Hydrogen Energy (INAFHE) would like to hold the coming IPST 2019 and 7th ICFCHT meeting on October 16 – 19, 2019 in Legian, Bali, Indonesia.

We are collaborating with other local associations, research groups and consortiums and covering the topics in Fibrous, Nanostructure Polymers, Blends and Composites, Polymer Technology in New and Renewable Energy, Smart and Functionalized Polymers, Advanced Synthesis Polymers, Green, Sustainable and Bio-polymers for the International Conference on IPST 2019 and Polymer Technology and Renewable Energy, Hydrogen and Fuel Cell –Various, Solid Oxide Fuel Cell and Materials and Others for the topics of 7th ICFCHT. Those topics will entirely cover the current emerging issues in trends of polymer science, fuel cell and hydrogen technology.

We look forward to meeting you in Bali, INDONESIA.
