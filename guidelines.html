Initial Manuscript Guideline

Please ensure that your conference paper satisfies the following points:

1. Originality 

Is the work scientifically rigorous, accurate and novel? Does the work contain significant additional material to that already published? Has its value been demonstrated?

You are agreeing to any necessary originality checks (plagiarism, multiple submissions) your paper may have to undergo during the peer review and production processes.  

Each manuscript must be accompanied by a statement that it has not been published elsewhere and that it has not been submitted simultaneously for publication elsewhere. Authors are responsible for obtaining permission to reproduce copyrighted material from other sources and are required to sign an agreement transferring the copyright to the publisher. All accepted manuscripts, artwork, and photographs become the property of the publisher. 

2. Relevancy 

Is the material appropriate to the scope of the conference to which it is submitted?

3. Clarity 

Is the English clear and well written? Poorly written English may obscure the scientific merit of your paper. Are the ideas expressed clearly and concisely? Are the concepts understandable?

How To Submit Your Manuscript

Manuscript file (ONLY in .doc or .docx) should be sent to the Guest Editor at EasyChair or at editorICFCHT@bppt.go.id

We wish you to be success with your publication.

Should you experience any difficulties during the preparation of your paper, please contact us at editorICFCHT@bppt.go.id for further information.

Guidelines for Oral Preparation and Presentation

Oral presentations (except plenary and keynote) have a 10 minutes slot. Please prepare a  PowerPoint (Microsoft Office 2007 or higher) presentation that lasts for 10 minutes. There will be a panel discussion for about 3-4 presenters that last for 5 minutes. The program chair will give you a warning at 8 minutes so you have to wrap up. The chair will stop you when your 10 minutes is up even if you have not finished. If you need any further information please do not hesitate to get in touch. 

Power Point presentation of all speakers must be delivered by email to editorICFCHT@bppt.go.id before 1 October 2019. If you wish to use your own laptop, please make it sure  and ready to work properly.  Please arrive at your session meeting room at least 15 minutes before the session begins. Take time to familiarize yourself with the set-up at the lectern. You will control/advance the slides during your presentation. Note: Wireless remotes are not provided as a standard item. 


Please check our website for the schedule of presentation.


Guidelines for Poster Preparation and Presentation

Poster Preparation 

 The X-banner poster with size of 1.60 m vertically and 0.60 m horizontally (portrait) should  be designed and prepared by the presenter and it would be placed in poster session area. The poster should be a readable presentation with good visual display. Please use the color for highlighting and to make your poster more attractive and also use pictures, diagrams, cartoons, figures, etc., rather than only text wherever possible. The poster can include an Abstract, if the author(s) think it is helpful. The information may conveniently be divided into sections, starting with an Introduction and ending with Conclusion. Other sections can be Objectives, Material and Methods, and Results. Acknowledgments can also be included. References can be included, if absolutely necessary. 

Poster Presentation 

The poster will have the poster code number as provided by the Organizer in the poster  format. Please check the code number and presentation schedule of your poster in the Program Book & Abstract. All posters should be displayed during the conference where the poster presentation will be held in two sessions. 

- Poster Session I (odd number): Wednesday, October 16th, 2019 at 14.30-15.00

- Poster Session II (even number): Thursday, October 17th , 2019 at 10.00-10.30 

The presenting author(s) must be present close to the poster during all the period assigned  and are expected to be available for questions at posters session. Poster presenters have to confirm their appearance to the secretariat during the registration time and then put the poster from Wednesday, October 16th, 2019. There will be Poster Awards in this event. To evaluate your poster by the judges, we ask you to send your poster in pdf to the secretariat at editorICFCHT@bppt.go.id before the event.
